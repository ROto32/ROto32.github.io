The Deep Love Between Pole and Jekco

In the quiet corners of a world so wide,
Where time slows down, and hearts collide,
There blooms a bond, unspoken, true,
Between Pole and Jekco, a love that grew.

Like rivers meet in the vast unknown,
Two souls connect, yet not alone.
With every glance, with every touch,
Their spirits entwine, they mean so much.

Pole, a force, strong and steady,
Jekco, a heart, ever ready.
Together they dance, together they rise,
Under the blanket of endless skies.

No storm could shake, no distance break,
The deep love they forever partake.
In silence, they speak, in laughter, they soar,
With every heartbeat, they love even more.

Their love is a melody, soft and grand,
A rhythm of life, hand in hand.
Through the days of joy and nights of sorrow,
Pole and Jekco, a love with no tomorrow.

For in their hearts, the universe spins,
A love that begins, and never ends.
